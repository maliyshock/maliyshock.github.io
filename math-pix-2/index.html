LaTeX, instantly.

Take a picture of your notes and get the LaTeX on your desktop in seconds. Mobile Snip syncs seamlessly with your desktop, so you can save even more time.

How does it work?

Whether on desktop or mobile, it takes only four simple steps.
  • Start by entering the keyboard shortcut
    Shortcuts on Macs, Windows, and Linux.
  • Capture your desired input
    Just click and drag to take a screenshot.
  • Your LaTeX is ready instantly
    Whichever LaTeX format you need is copied automatically to your clipboard.
  • Paste the LaTeX into any compatible editor
    You can easily configure your LaTeX formats and delimiters in your app Settings.
  • Go to the Camera mode
    You can find it at the bottom left of your screen.
  • Fit the box around your equation
    Just tap, drag, and release!
  • The LaTeX syncs with your desktop
    Snip results sync with all devices linked to your Mathpix account.
  • Paste the LaTeX into any compatible editor
    Customize your LaTeX formats and delimiters, so you have exactly what you need, with every snip.
