Data-driven Insights

Would you like to compare thousands of models of aircraft on price, operating cost, performance, and safety?

Planephd is the only place you can do that.

Questions no one can answer

  • Should I buy a new plane or one that's 20 years old?

  • What’s the total cost of ownership of each? How does the resale liquidity compare?

  • Are they equally safe?

  • How likely am I to make it to TBO on this model?

  • Am I paying too much for insurance?

  • Who has experience replacing the windshield and what will it cost?

We can even tell you if your vintage aircraft was built when your registration claims.

It's amazing what we learned when we connected all the dots.

How did we do it?

Planephd has the largest aircraft database ever assembled. We started with data on over 700,000 aircraft, and then using advanced statistical models, artificial intelligence, and financial techniques, we cleaned and built up that data to become something much greater.

But that data is meaningless without a community.

This is a community

We built planephd so that you could make it grow. It learns every day from its users. If you want improved transparency for the costs, safety, and reliability of aircraft, products and services, become a part of the solution.

Sign up today. It’s free. It’s fun.
